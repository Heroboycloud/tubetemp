Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro and Team plans are billed monthly or yearly and can be cancelled anytime — you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What counts as an "analysis"?

Each time you paste a video URL and TubeTemp fetches and scores its comments, that's one analysis.

Do you store the comments you analyze?

Free plan analyses aren't saved. Pro and Team plans keep a history so you can revisit past results without re-running them.
